Who I Am

More than just an assistant

I am Mwendwa Patricia Nzambi, an AI-enabled Executive Virtual Assistant based in Kenya, specializing in support for CEOs, C-suite leaders, and real estate & property management executives.

My journey into virtual assistance started through real-world, hands-on experience — not a classroom. This gave me firsthand exposure to what executives actually need: clear communication, flawless organization, and proactive support. I later formalized my skills through the prestigious ALX Africa EVA certification (February–April 2026).

I've since built AI tools and automation platforms like Zapier, Notion, and ChatGPT into how I work — not because it's trendy, but because it means I can do in an hour what used to take a full day. That extra time goes straight back to the executives and businesses I support.

I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Business IT and Project Management at Jomo Kenyatta University of Science and Technology, deepening my technical and strategic capabilities every day. I thrive in fast-paced remote environments and bring a culture of confidentiality, professionalism, and initiative to every engagement.
